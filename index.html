Streaming radio app

Styled stations, background playback, and a mobile-first radio experience.

GTA Radio is a themed streaming radio app built for fans of station surfing, strong visual identity, and simple playback on mobile. The app features curated station lineups, artwork-rich browsing, and a polished listening experience that keeps moving when the app goes into the background.

About The App

This site is the public home for GTA Radio. It provides a simple overview of the app, a support contact point, and access to the current privacy policy for users, collaborators, and distribution partners.

Station-driven design

Each station uses its own visual personality rather than a one-size-fits-all shell, helping the app feel closer to channel surfing than generic streaming.

Background listening

The listening experience is designed to continue when the app is not in the foreground, keeping the radio feel intact while users move through the rest of their day.

Admin-managed content

Tracks, artwork, and menu ordering are managed through a separate admin workflow so the public app can stay clean, lightweight, and focused on listening.

Core Features

The app is currently centered around streaming radio-style playback, themed station pages, and a hosted content system that allows the experience to evolve over time.
