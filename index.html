GTA Radio Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 30, 2026

GTA Radio ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and shared when you use the GTA Radio mobile application.

1. Information We May Collect

GTA Radio does not require users to create an account to listen to stations. However, certain information may be processed when you use the app.

  • Device and app information, such as device model, operating system, app version, language, and basic diagnostics
  • Network and usage information needed to stream audio and load app content, such as IP address and requests made to our servers
  • Advertising-related information collected by our ad provider, including advertising identifiers, device identifiers, approximate location, and ad interaction data, depending on your device settings and applicable law

2. How We Use Information

  • Deliver radio content and station assets
  • Operate, maintain, and improve the app
  • Diagnose technical issues and app errors
  • Display advertisements
  • Measure ad performance and app usage
  • Protect the app and related services against abuse or unauthorized access

3. Advertising

GTA Radio uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google and its partners may collect and use certain data to provide, personalize, and measure ads.

This may include advertising identifiers, device information, approximate location, and app interaction or ad engagement data.

For more information, review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services

4. Audio Streaming and Content Delivery

When you use GTA Radio, the app connects to our content services to load station menus, track information, artwork, and audio streams. These requests may involve technical information such as IP address and device-related request data needed to deliver the service.

5. Data Sharing

We may share information:

  • With service providers that help us operate the app, including hosting and advertising providers
  • When required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request
  • To protect our rights, users, systems, or services

We do not sell user personal information in the ordinary operation of the app.

6. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the app, provide services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

7. Children's Privacy

GTA Radio is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and address the issue.

8. Your Choices

Depending on your device and region, you may be able to limit personalized advertising through your device settings or consent controls provided by advertising services.

You can also stop using the app at any time.

9. Security

We use reasonable measures to help protect information processed through the app. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes become effective means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.
